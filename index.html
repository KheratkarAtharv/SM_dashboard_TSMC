Environmental, Social & Governance Excellence

TSMC is committed to sustainable semiconductor manufacturing through comprehensive ESG initiatives.

Environmental Impact

Carbon Neutrality Goal

TSMC aims for net-zero carbon emissions by 2050, with intermediate targets to reduce emissions 40% by 2030.

  • Renewable energy investment of $2.8 billion
  • Target: 100% renewable energy by 2030
  • Currently: ~33% renewable energy usage

Water Resource Management

Sustainable water usage in semiconductor manufacturing with advanced recycling systems.

  • Reduction of water consumption by 30%
  • 70% water recycling rate
  • First fab to achieve ISO 46001 certification

Waste Management

Comprehensive waste reduction and recycling programs across all operations.

  • 99.2% waste recycling rate
  • Zero landfill operations
  • Hazardous waste properly managed

Renewable Energy

Aggressive transition to renewable energy sources across manufacturing facilities.

  • Solar and wind installations
  • Green power purchase agreements
  • Energy efficiency improvements: 3% annually
